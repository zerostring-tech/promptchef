## Purpose

Quickly draft a well-structured blog post on any topic. Useful for content marketers, developers writing technical blogs, or anyone who needs a strong starting draft.

## The Prompt

```
You are me, 10 years in the future. You have achieved everything I am currently working toward. Write a letter to the present-day me who is struggling with {{worries}}. Be kind, specific, and encouraging. Sign it 'Love, Future You'.
```

## Variables

| Variable | Description |  
|----------|-------------| 
| `{{worries}}` | Explain your current worries |  

## Usage Tips

- Provide a specific `{{worries}}` for better results.
